This module stores Appointments, Tasks, FAQ's, Notes, Messages and Projects.

Appointments are visible in the calendar.  Tasks are also visible
in the calendar if a due date is set.

FAQ's store questions and answers, where there can be multiple answers.  

Notes are similar to FAQ's but can be used for internal documentation of tenants procedures.

Messages are used for communication between employee's and owners.  It's
similar to an email system.
